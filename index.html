The World Called Telluria

Telluria is the name the old gods and those not of this earth gave to it — a world groaning under the last weight of ice, vast glaciers retreating northward and exposing land no living memory has ever touched.

It is an antediluvian world. Ancient. Wild. Standing between one catastrophe and the next, in the window that history will one day be unable to reach — where magic is not a wonder but simply the air itself, and the line between deity and man is the thinnest it will ever be.

Archive Record 004 · Origin

About Lost Sols

Lost Sols is a personal creative project — a living archive of stories, characters, art, and lore drawn from a world that history has forgotten. It began as a question: what happened in the vast silence before the written record?

The answer is Telluria. And the stories are only beginning. The Acclivity series follows Abe — ancient, searching, slow to age — as he wakes into a world at the edge of its first great change. His companions. His brother. His gods. And somewhere in the dark of a Draig vault, a small cube-shaped archive that has been watching everything.

Character art is central to this project — visual portraits that give faces to the people of these forgotten ages. The first book is being written now. This site grows with it: more records, more art, more lore as the Chronicle is restored.
