ARCHIVE RECORD 004 · ORIGIN

About Lost Sols

Lost Sols is a personal creative project — a living archive of stories, characters, art, and lore drawn from a world that history has forgotten. It began as a question: what happened in the vast silence before the written record? What lived, loved, and was lost in the ages that left no text behind?

The answer is Telluria. And the stories are only beginning. The Acclivity series follows Abe — ancient, searching, slow to age — as he wakes into a world at the edge of its first great change. His companions. His brother. His gods. And somewhere in the dark of a Draig vault, a small cube-shaped archive that has been watching everything.

Character art is central to this project — visual portraits that give faces to the people of these forgotten ages. The first book is being written now. This site grows with it: more records, more art, more lore as the Chronicle is restored.
